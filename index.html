DUBTRACK YOUTUBE PLAYLIST IMPORTER (beta)

Simple Youtube Importer for Dubtrack

Project on GitHub Inside my global Dubtrack repository.
Bookmark Dubtrack Youtube Playlist Importer (Drag to your bookmarks)
How to enable Click on the bookmark once you're in Dubtrack.
How to import from public playlist
  • Go to Dubtrack, open the browser console pressing F12. If you're not on the "console" tab then go there.
  • Copy and paste to console: YTImport.import('{name of youtube account that owns the playlist}', '{name of playlist in youtube}', '{name of playlist in dubtrack}');
  • Replace the stuff inside curly brackets with what it tells you to (also remove the curly brackets).
  • Hit Enter
  • ??? (wait for it to export)
  • Profit!
How to import from private playlist
  • Get the playlist url, it should look like this: https://youtube.com/playlist?list={a bunch of characters}
  • Copy the "bunch of characters"
  • Go to Dubtrack, open browser console pressing F12. If you're not on the "console" tab then go there.
  • Copy and paste to console: YTImport.importFromPlaylistId('{id of playlist in youtube}', '{name of playlist in dubtrack}');
  • Replace the stuff in curly brackets that says "{id of playlist in youtube} with the characters you copied before (thats the playlist ID!).
  • Replace the stuff inside curly brackets with what it tells you to (also remove the curly brackets).
  • Hit Enter
  • ??? (wait for it to export)
  • Profit!
